Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Japanese Tutor ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Identifier – A randomly generated UUID created on first launch and stored on your device. It is never linked to your name, email address, or Apple ID.
  • User Settings – Your chosen proficiency level, topics, regions, writing system, and other learning preferences.
  • Learning Data – Articles you have read, vocabulary words you have studied, quiz results, and progress statistics.
  • App Preferences – Theme, display, and notification settings.

We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized Japanese learning articles based on your preferences.
  • To track your vocabulary progress and avoid repeating content you have already studied.
  • To provide quiz and statistics features.
  • To improve the quality of AI-generated content over time.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic Claude API – Article and quiz content is generated using Anthropic's Claude AI model. Prompts sent to Claude may include your proficiency level and topic preferences but never personally identifiable information. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Google Cloud Firestore – Your settings and learning data are stored in Google Cloud Firestore. Data is associated only with your random device UUID. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Apple App Store & StoreKit – Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store payment information. Apple's privacy policy is available at apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as the app is installed on your device and you continue to use the service. You can request deletion of all data associated with your device UUID by contacting us at the address below.

5. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Your device UUID is not shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

Japanese Tutor is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of data associated with your device UUID at any time by contacting us. Because the device UUID is not linked to your identity, you will need to provide it (available in Settings → Device ID) when contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
