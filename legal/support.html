Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate a new article?

Open the Articles tab and tap the generate button (top right). Article generation takes 20–30 seconds because the AI searches for real current news and writes a custom learning article for you.

What is my proficiency level (JLPT)?

JLPT N5 is beginner (basic vocabulary and grammar), and N1 is near-native fluency. If you are just starting out, choose N5. You can change this at any time in Settings → Proficiency Level.

What writing systems are available?

Romaji – Articles written in the Latin alphabet using Hepburn romanization. Best for complete beginners.
Kana (ひらがな) – Articles written in hiragana/katakana. Good for intermediate learners.
Kanji (漢字) – Articles written with kanji and furigana. Best for advanced learners.

Can I change my topics and regions?

Yes. Go to Settings and edit the Topics and Regions lists. The AI will focus on news from those areas. You can add up to 10 topics and 10 regions.

My article generation failed. What should I do?

This can happen if the server is temporarily busy or there is a network issue. Please wait 30 seconds and try again. If the problem persists, check that the API Base URL in Settings is correct and contact support.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your name → Subscriptions.
3. Tap Japanese Tutor Premium.
4. Tap Cancel Subscription.

You will retain access until the end of the current billing period. Refunds are handled by Apple; contact Apple Support at reportaproblem.apple.com.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings → Subscription → Manage Subscription inside the app and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Where is my data stored?

Your articles, vocabulary, and settings are stored in Google Cloud Firestore and are associated only with an anonymous device ID (shown in Settings → Device ID). No personally identifiable information is stored. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

Contact us with your Device ID (found in Settings → Device ID) and we will delete all data associated with it within 7 business days.
