Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate an article?

Tap the "Generate" button on the main screen. The app will find a recent Japanese news story and create a learning article matched to your JLPT level.

Why does article generation take a while?

Each article is freshly created by AI from real news — this involves searching for a current story, writing the article, building vocabulary lists, and generating quiz questions. It usually takes about 30–60 seconds.

Can I change my JLPT level?

Yes. Go to Settings in the app and select your preferred JLPT level (N5–N1). New articles will be tailored to the level you choose.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Open Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad. You can change or cancel your plan at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to submit a refund request.
