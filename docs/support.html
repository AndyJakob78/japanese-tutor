Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate a new article?

Open the Articles tab and tap the generate button in the top-right corner. Article generation takes 20–30 seconds because the AI searches for real current news and writes a custom learning article tailored to your level.

What JLPT level should I choose?

JLPT N5 is beginner (basic vocabulary and grammar) and N1 is near-native fluency. If you are just starting out, choose N5. You can change your level at any time in Settings → Proficiency Level.

What writing systems are available?

Romaji — Latin alphabet (Hepburn romanisation). Best for complete beginners.
Kana (ひらがな) — Hiragana and katakana. Good for intermediate learners.
Kanji (漢字) — Kanji with furigana. Best for advanced learners.

Change your writing system in Settings → Writing System.

Can I choose my topics and regions?

Yes. Go to Settings and edit the Topics and Regions lists. The AI will focus on news from those areas. You can add up to 10 topics and 10 regions.

Article generation failed — what do I do?

This can happen when the server is temporarily busy or there is a network issue. Wait 30 seconds and try again. If the problem persists, check that the API Base URL in Settings is correct, then contact support below.

How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your name → Subscriptions.
3. Tap Nihongo News Premium.
4. Tap Cancel Subscription.

You keep access until the end of the current billing period. Refund requests go to Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings → Subscription → Manage Subscription in the app and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Where is my data stored?

Your articles, vocabulary, and settings are stored in Google Cloud Firestore and are associated only with an anonymous device ID (visible in Settings → Device ID). No personally identifiable information is stored. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my data?

Contact us (below) with your Device ID (found in Settings → Device ID) and we will delete all associated data within 7 business days.
